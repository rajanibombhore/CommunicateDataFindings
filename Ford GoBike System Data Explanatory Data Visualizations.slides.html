Bike Rentals(Bay Area) Trends (Year- 2019)¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, We will explore the trends of bike rentals and predict the high bike rental seasons. The main focus will be the bike rental times, which can be split into time of day, week and month. We will further explore by the user types i.e., by Customer or Subscriber.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consists of 2506983 records of bike rental data. The dataset includes the rental details such as the duration of rental, start and end station details, bike id's and the user details. The rental_access_method is ignored because this was introduced mid year.

Distribution of bike rentals by month for the year 2019. The highest month of rentals being July and lowest being December.

Bike Rental by month for each user type (Customer vs Subscriber)¶

The bike rental trends here are plotted for both the user types. December was the lowest for all the bike rentals, Whereas when plotting the trends for each user type, we can see the maximum bike rentals for Customers being December. The maximum bike rentals for a Subscriber is in the month of March and July being the second most.

Bike Rental by week for each user type(Customer vs Subscriber)¶

Here we observe that the bike rentals for Subscribers are high on the weekdays and low on the weekends. The bike rentals are popular on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays for the Customers.

Bike rental by time of day and month for each user type¶

We can observe the popular time of bike rentals for Customers in the month of December(Afternoon, Evening and Morning) and September and October(In the evenings). Similarly the popular time of bike rentals for Subscribers are in the months of March, April and July in the Evenings. Also, the Subscribers have a high frequency of bike rentals throughout the year in the evenings.
